## Remaining runtime evidence

Before ESP32-S3 runtime performance can be accepted:

- obtain explicit authorization to upload and execute the benchmark on the
  exact board;
- capture 30 CSV trials after warm-up for disabled, all-due, mixed-rate, and
  maximum-capacity workloads;
- record counter overhead, median, p95, worst, heap delta, and stack high-water
  mark;
- compare tick counts with expected semantics and investigate every mismatch;
- record the exact hardware/carrier board, power setup, source commit, and
  captured output.

Compile evidence must not be promoted to runtime or physical-hardware claims.
